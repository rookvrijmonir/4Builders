FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly does a full renovation involve?
In a full renovation, we address the entire property — from foundation and structure to roof, facades, installations and finishing. You get a single point of contact who coordinates the entire process, so you don't have to worry about a thing.
Do you have experience with heritage buildings and canal houses?
Yes, we specialise in renovations of heritage buildings and canal houses in Amsterdam. We understand the specific requirements for heritage permits and work with materials and techniques that respect the historical character of the property.
Do you also arrange the permits?
Absolutely. We handle all necessary permits, from building permits to heritage permits and parking exemptions for material deliveries. Complete peace of mind is our starting point.
How does the process begin?
It starts with a free consultation. We discuss your wishes, assess the current state of the property and create a plan of action. You then receive a detailed quote with clear phasing and scheduling.
Why are there no prices listed on the website?
Every renovation is unique. The price depends on the current state of the property, the desired scope, heritage status and accessibility. We prefer to provide an honest, detailed quote tailored to your specific situation.
