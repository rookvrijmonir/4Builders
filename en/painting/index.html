FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does exterior painting typically take?
This depends on the scope and condition of the woodwork. For an average property with window frames, expect 3-5 working days. With wood rot or extensive preparation work, this may be longer. We always provide a realistic timeline upfront.
Which paints do you use?
We work exclusively with premium brands such as Sigma and Sikkens. These paints offer superior coverage, colour retention, and durability. We recommend the optimal solution for your specific situation.
Do you handle smaller projects as well?
Absolutely. Whether it is a single window frame or a complete property, we are delighted to assist. Contact us for a complimentary, no-obligation quote.
Do you work with property associations?
Yes, we have extensive experience with property association projects. From stairwells to shared facades and multi-year maintenance plans. We ensure clear communication with all stakeholders involved.
