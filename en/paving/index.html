FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you also work on small courtyards?
Yes, particularly in Amsterdam we frequently work on compact gardens and terraces with challenging access. We have extensive experience with narrow passages, rear entrances and stairs - we plan the logistics carefully.
Can you repair subsidence?
Yes, we lift the tiles, restore the sand bed and relay everything neatly. The edge finishing is also properly secured again.
What if water keeps pooling?
This usually indicates incorrect gradient or drainage. We assess the situation on-site and provide a practical solution with the appropriate drainage system.
How long does an average paving project take?
This depends on the scope and ground conditions. An average terrace is typically completed within 2-4 working days. Larger projects or extensive groundwork may take longer. We always provide a realistic timeline in advance.
