FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does an HOA project work with your company?
After an initial inspection, we provide a detailed quote. Upon approval, we schedule the work in consultation with the board and inform all residents. During the project, we keep the board updated on progress, and after completion, we conduct a final walkthrough together.
Can you help develop a long-term maintenance plan?
Absolutely. We are happy to assist with prioritizing and planning maintenance. We can conduct thorough inspections and advise on which maintenance is needed when, helping you optimize your reserve funds effectively.
How do you minimize disruption to residents?
We work efficiently and maintain a clean workspace, tidying up daily and ensuring common areas remain accessible. Residents are informed in advance about the work schedule and any temporary inconveniences.
Are you insured for HOA projects?
Yes, we carry comprehensive business liability insurance. Upon request, we can provide a certificate of insurance for your records and administrative purposes.
