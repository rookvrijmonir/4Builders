FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you create bespoke built-in wardrobes and cabinets?
Absolutely. Especially in Amsterdam, built-in solutions are often the smartest choice. We create perfectly fitted pieces that work beautifully, even in challenging spaces with uneven walls and characterful heritage properties.
Can you deliver a paint-ready finish?
Certainly. We prepare all surfaces to a high standard so that subsequent paintwork can be completed to perfection. All joints and transitions are finished with precision.
Do you undertake smaller projects?
Yes, provided it fits within our schedule. Describe your project clearly and we will promptly advise what to expect. From a single door installation to complete interior finishing.
Why are there no prices listed on the website?
Custom work, existing conditions and accessibility all influence the final price. We prefer to provide an accurate, competitive quote rather than generic estimates that may not reflect your specific requirements.
