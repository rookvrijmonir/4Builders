FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you also provide floor leveling services?
Yes. Especially with luxury vinyl (and many Amsterdam floors) leveling is often essential for achieving a flawless result. We assess the substrate and advise whether leveling is necessary.
Can you install baseboards?
Absolutely. Skirting boards complete the look - we install them precisely and finish all joints impeccably. We supply and install various types of baseboards according to your preferences.
How long does floor installation take?
This depends on the size and condition of the substrate. An average apartment of 60-80m2 is typically completed in 1-2 days. If leveling is required, add one additional day for drying time.
Do you work with landlords and HOAs?
Yes. A new floor is one of the quickest ways to refresh a property for rental or sale. We have extensive experience with HOA requirements regarding sound insulation and underlay specifications.
